ABOUT US

“Business is not an activity but a religion.”

 

 

Providing best-in-class services to our customers is a business mantra of the company.

 

Amidst the rich heritage and history of Rajasthan, stands tall and proud the "Blue City" Jodhpur situated in the Thar Desert. Away from busy city life, in the peaceful vicinity where nature resides, we welcome you to Umalaxmi Organics Pvt. Ltd. An organic ecosystem is the key to healthy living & we at Umalaxmi endeavors to create the same by providing world-class services & products to our customers. With over 20 years of experience, our team ensures that our valued customers are provided with authentic products only. To maintain the authenticity & purity of our products regular in house audits & quality controls are applied at each step starting from the farms under cultivation, harvesting & plucking, transportation and processing of raw materials at our state-of-the-Art extraction plants equipped with the best technology to produce quality products.
