RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

Our Research & Development (R&D) centre is like the central nervous system of our company, performing independently in Pharmacology, Microbiology, Bio-assay, Formulation & Development and Quality Control laboratories, which synchronizes together with synergy and unique co-ordination at various stages of product development.

  • Our R&D programs are USFDA COMPLIANT and adhere to strict guidelines established by WHO, TGA and others.
  • Our team of professionals & experts is vigilant in delivering products backed by scientific, pharmacological, safety and toxicity studies. Our R&D team works hard to bring about improvisations in the existing range of products. They keep themselves up-to-date with the constant changes in the industry and incorporate their evaluations and market assessments while conceiving and developing a product. Our entire workforce believes in working in close coordination with our clients in order to understand their requirements. They conform to the established quality standards to develop our range of products right from the extraction of herbs to the final packaging of the product.
  • A well-equipped, state of the art, in-house laboratory beautified with high-tech instruments like HPLCs, GCs, and UV & Spectrophotometer, are used to comply with the accurate detection and measurement, so that it that it meets with the customer’s specification and satisfaction. Our test methods are validated by USP, EP, BP & JP.
  • Our R & D team has perfected methods of identifying each botanical & natural ingredient for its color, texture, taste and purity, microbiological and heavy metals analysis & measuring active constituents.

Umalaxmi’s top management happily spends a lion’s share of its time, money and energy for research and development of its botanical & natural products. Exploring how a raw material can be maximally used with preserving its innate constituents is of major interest to us.
