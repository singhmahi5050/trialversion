UMALAXMI PROGRESS

2000

Umalaxmi International was established in Gujarat as an Export Trading House of Pharmaceutical and Botanical extracts.

2004

Obtained USDA Organic Certiﬁcation for processing Senna, Psyllium, Ashwagandha, Sesame Seeds.

2005

Established the 1st Herbal Extraction Plant (750,000 sq. ft.) in Jodhpur and began exporting Boswellia, Ashwagandha, Senna Extracts etc., and also became ISO, GMP, HACCP, USDA Organic Certified Company.

2012

Pioneered with Branded Products like Sugar Remedy, Mega Kutki, and many more.

2015

2nd new plant was established, which was dedicated 100% to Organic Ethanol Extracts in 35,000sq.ft. area.

2018

Obtained Certification from KFDA for Organic Moringa and installed 3rd Plant for Herbs and Spice Powder.

2022

Launch of CO2 Extraction Plant.
